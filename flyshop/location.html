Location

Grindstone Fly Shop Location

The fly shop is ideally located for easy access from Toronto, the Windsor/Detroit, Niagara Falls, and New York borders, and is only 45 minutes west of Toronto's Pearson International airport.

Waterdown is centrally located in the heart of south central Ontario, within easy driving distance of three of the Great Lakes and their tributary rivers. If it's migratory brown trout, steelhead or salmon you're after; call for prime season information: (Mar to May or Sept to Dec)
