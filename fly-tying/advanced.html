Advanced Fly Tying

Fly tying secrets are shared and complex processes are explained to tie the most challenging patterns. The class covers the more complex and difficult techniques such as: spinning deer hair, winging for dry flies, weaving, epoxy and introduction to tube flies. This session will complete the Grindstone course list but practice is still required to perfect these skills.
